Pops Table Design

POPS END TABLE

Pops is an elegant and functional sofa side table. Its sinuous shape allows the base to slide seamlessly under the sofa, integrating perfectly into the space without ever weighing it down. The project is designed to highlight the craftsmanship that distinguishes Carpanelli: the expertly curved wood interacts with the glass top, creating a refined contrast between materiality and lightness. Every detail is carefully conceived to enhance quality, achieving a perfect balance between aesthetics and functionality. Pops is the ideal accent piece for a contemporary living space that values design without compromising on comfort.
