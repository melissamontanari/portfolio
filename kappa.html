Kappa Table Design

Kappa Table — concept & specs

The Kappa table is a tribute to the artisanal excellence that has always defined the Carpanelli brand. Designed to celebrate fine woodworking through fluid and harmonious forms, Kappa unites aesthetics and structure in perfect balance. Its sculpted base, crafted with complex techniques and refined finishes, becomes the focal point of the space, enhancing the natural elegance of wood as a living, noble material. Conceived for elegant and contemporary interiors, this piece tells the story of craftsmanship, interpreting tradition through an innovative and modern lens.

USE  Dining table
DIMENSIONS  210 × 110 × 75 cm
MATERIALS  Solid wood, marble, glass
FINISHES  Natural wood, lacquered glass, polished marble
COLOURS  Natural wood, white, black
