Showroom Design for Italgraniti

Italgraniti

The project involves the development of a new showroom for Italgraniti Group, a leading Italian company in the high-end porcelain stoneware sector, internationally recognized for its design quality, technological innovation, and commitment to environmental sustainability. The showroom is conceived as an exhibition space that fully reflects the companyâ€™s values, with a strong focus on product excellence. Special attention is given to the creative reuse of production waste materials, reinterpreted as architectural and scenographic elements, demonstrating a responsible design approach. The project is located in the Valchiavenna area, within the municipality of Samolaco, a natural setting characterized by a typically Alpine landscape.

Our concept strives for excellence in product presentation, leveraging both natural and artificial light to enhance every detail. The main goal is to create an engaging visual experience for customers, allowing them to fully appreciate the quality and aesthetics of the products, inspired by the tradition and beauty of the mountains and nature. Natural light is emphasized through the use of perimeter walls made from waste materials supplied by Italgraniti. These elements intersect with one another, leaving gaps and slits that allow light to filter through, creating evocative plays of shadow and reflection within the exhibition space.
