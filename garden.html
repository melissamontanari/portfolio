Kindergarten Project The Garden of Wonders

The Garden of Wonders

The project involves the construction of a nursery and kindergarten designed to accommodate 60 children, divided into two age groups: 0–3 and 3–5 years. The assigned plot has a total area of 11,750 m2, with a buildable area of 4,300 m2. One of the main challenges was to combine creativity and design sensitivity with compliance with current educational regulations, ensuring safe, functional, and stimulating spaces for children’s growth and learning.

The project was created with the aim of offering an inclusive, multisensory, and creative experience. The sensory greenhouses are the heart of the project: four spaces dedicated to touch, sight, taste, and smell. A fifth greenhouse hosts a vegetable garden, where children can learn about the natural cycle. The interior spaces are organized into themed rooms that enrich the educational journey. For nursery-aged children, there is a culture room, a reading room, and a nap room. For kindergarten children, there is an art room, a music room, and a science room. The outdoor area includes a space for pet therapy with horses and a large play area.
